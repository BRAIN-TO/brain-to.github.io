# {% include icon.html icon="fa-solid fa-users" %}Open Positions

For all positions, we encourage people from diverse backgrounds to apply. Our lab strives to be a welcoming place for everyone, and we value equity, diversity and inclusivity highly. Come and join us to make us better at it!

We currently have the following positions to be filled:

## Post-doctoral Fellow
We are looking for a highly motivated post-doctoral fellow to join our interdisciplinary team. The successful candidate will work on cutting-edge neuroimaging research projects, collaborating with experts in the field. Applicants should have a strong background in neuroimaging, data analysis, and programming skills. Experience with machine learning and statistical modeling is a plus. Interested candidates should send their CV, a cover letter, and contact information for three references to [kamil.uludag@utoronto.ca](mailto:kamil.uludag@utoronto.ca).

## Graduate Students (PhD and MSc)
We welcome applications from prospective graduate students interested in pursuing research in neuroimaging and related fields. Our lab offers a supportive and collaborative environment for students to develop their skills and contribute to impactful research. Interested applicants should contact [kamil.uludag@utoronto.ca](mailto:kamil.uludag@utoronto.ca).

A lab rotation is the best way for you to get to know us and vice versa. Please get in touch with us, even if your dream project is not listed here. We are always excited to get to know bright and enthusiastic students.

## Undergraduate Students
We often have opportunities for motivated undergraduate students to complete various trainee or volunteer positions with our lab. These include but are not limited to thesis or research projects for course credit, as well as placements such as work-study (UofT), internship/ placement, or co-op. Please note that it is the applicant's responsibility to ensure that they meet the requirements and deadlines for any potential position.

Please send your CV, academic transcript, and outline your interest in joining the lab to [kamil.uludag@utoronto.ca](mailto:kamil.uludag@utoronto.ca).
