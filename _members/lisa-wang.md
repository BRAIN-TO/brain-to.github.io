Lisa Wang is an MSc student in the Department of Medical Biophysics at University of Toronto. She received her Bachelor of Applied Science degree in 2023, majoring in Mechanical Engineering with minors in Bioengineering and Mechatronics, and a certificate in Artificial Intelligence Engineering. Her research focuses on numerical simulation of navigator-based prospective motion correction in magnetic resonance imaging (MRI), with the goal of guiding the design of MR navigators that mitigate image degradation caused by patient motion during scanning. Outside of research, Lisa enjoys travelling, music, art, and food.
