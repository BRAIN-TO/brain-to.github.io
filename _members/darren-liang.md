Darren Liang joined University Health Network in December 2025. He received his B.Sc. in psychology from the University of Toronto and later an M.Sc. from Western University. His work has varied in academia and industry spanning clinical research, statistics, and neuroimaging techniques where he honed his skills at institutions including Baycrest, Western University, and CAMH. Aside from his research specialty using neuroimaging in MEG and 3T MRI across clinical populations, he is currently supporting the BRAIN-TO lab through project and research management. In his spare time, Darren enjoys exploring new places and keeping up with current events.
