Darren Liang joined University Health Network in December 2025. He received his B.Sc. in psychology from the University of Toronto and the later M.Sc. from Western University. His work has varied in academia and industry spanning clinical research, statistics, and working with various neuroimaging techniques. He has experience from working various research insitutions including Baycrest, Western University, and CAMH using neuroimaging in 3T across clinical populations. in Currently he is supporting the research at the BRAIN-TO through  project management. In his spare time, Darren enjoys exploring new places and keeping up with current events.
